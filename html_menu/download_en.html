Game description InfoDef


Developed game InfoDef designed to raise awareness people, company employees in the field of information technology. Understanding the principles and basics of cyber security, to introduce user with the main types of threats. Application completed in the form of tests, which also allows for an assessment of knowledge from the basics cyber security.The program works under many modern operating systems linux android windows. Application of the program on smartphones for android makes it impossible use your smartphone as a source for finding answers when testing (which will be useful for educational institutions). Also time counter entered, implemented hidden auto score in the form of smiles and scoring.
